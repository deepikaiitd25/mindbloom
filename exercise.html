🧘 Move your body, calm your mind

Exercise releases endorphins — your brain's natural mood lifters. Even 10 minutes helps.

🌬️ Breathing Exercises

Do these anytime you feel anxious, overwhelmed, or stressed.

🫁

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale slowly for 8. Repeat 4 times. This activates your body's calm response almost instantly.

2 minutesAnytimeReduces anxiety
📦

Box Breathing

Inhale 4 → Hold 4 → Exhale 4 → Hold 4. Repeat. Used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure — works for exam stress too!

5 minutesBefore examsImproves focus
🌊

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Notice 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Instantly brings you back to the present moment.

3 minutesWhen overwhelmedGrounds you

🧘 Yoga & Stretching

Gentle movements to release tension stored in your body.

🙆

Child's Pose (Balasana)

Kneel and fold forward, arms extended or resting beside you. Hold for 1–2 minutes. Melts away back tension and calms the nervous system.

2 minutesBeginnerCalms nerves
🌲

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Stand on one leg, place the other foot on your inner thigh. Hands in prayer. Balance for 30 seconds each side. Builds focus and stability.

3 minutesBeginnerImproves focus
😴

Legs Up the Wall

Lie on your back, legs resting up a wall. Stay 5–10 minutes. One of the most restorative poses — perfect before sleep.

10 minutesBefore sleepRelieves stress
