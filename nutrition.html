🧠 Why food affects mental health

The science behind eating well for your mind.

🫀

Gut-Brain Connection

About 90% of serotonin is made in your gut. Good food means a better mood.

⚡

Stable Blood Sugar

Skipping meals causes energy crashes and mood swings. Eat regularly.

🧬

Brain Nutrients

Omega-3s, Vitamin B, Magnesium and Zinc directly support brain health.

💧

Hydration

Even mild dehydration causes fatigue and irritability. Drink 8 glasses a day.
