🧠 Why does food affect mental health?

🫀

Gut-Brain Connection

About 90% of serotonin (the "happy chemical") is made in your gut. Good food = better mood.

⚡

Stable Blood Sugar

Skipping meals causes energy crashes and mood swings. Eat regularly to stay emotionally balanced.

🧬

Brain Nutrients

Omega-3s, Vitamin B, Magnesium, and Zinc directly support brain function and reduce anxiety.

💧

Hydration

Even mild dehydration causes fatigue, poor concentration, and irritability. Drink 8 glasses a day.
